Upcoming Movies

Oppenheimer (2023)

Christopher Nolan's upcoming biopic about J. Robert Oppenheimer and the creation of the atomic bomb. The film stars Cillian Murphy as Oppenheimer and joining him is an ensemble cast of Emily Blunt, Matt Damon, and Robert Downey Jr.

Asteroid City (2023)

An upcoming Wes Anderson film set in a small American town in the desert. The film features a large ensemble cast including Tom Hanks, Scarlett Johansson, Bryan Cranston, Steve Carell, Margot Robbie, and Willem Dafoe.

Indiana Jones and the Dial of Destiny (2023)

The fifth and final installment of the Indiana Jones franchise will be set during the space race. Joining Harrison Ford in the cast are Phoebe Waller-Bridge and Mads Mikkelsen.
